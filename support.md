## Getting Started

When you open the app, you’ll start on the **New Chat** page. This is where you can create chats with the AI models you’ve downloaded.

To begin, tap **Load Model** and choose a downloaded model to chat with.

If you don’t have any models available yet, close the Load Model page and open the menu by tapping the **hamburger icon** in the top-left corner. From the menu, tap **ModelsHub**.

## Downloading a Model

**ModelsHub** is where you can browse, download, and manage AI models. You can think of it like an app store for AI models.

Tap the **Download Hugging Face Model** button to open the Hugging Face Hub.

From there, you can either choose one of the suggested models or search for a compatible model. Good starter options include:

- **Qwen 3.5 2B**
- **SmolLM3 3B**

> **Note:** Currently, models must be in **MLX format** and supported by the app in order to run.

Once the download is complete, tap the **hamburger icon** again, return to **New Chat**, load your model, and start chatting.

## Common Questions

### Does the app require internet?
Internet is required to download models. Once a compatible model is downloaded, the app can run it locally on your device without an internet connection.

### Where are my prompts processed and chats saved?

Once you have downloaded a compatible model, all inference is performed locally on your device.

Your saved conversations can be viewed on the **Previous Chats** page, where you can rename or delete chats at any time.

**Temporary chats** are not saved by default.
