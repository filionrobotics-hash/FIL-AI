# FIL AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-05-06

FIL AI is developed by FILIONROBOTICS.

## Information We Collect

FIL AI does not require users to create an account. FIL AI does not sell user data or use user data for advertising or tracking.

Chats with AI models are stored locally on the user’s device. Users can delete their conversations at any time from within the app. If Temporary Chat is enabled, conversations are not saved after the chat session ends.

When you use ModelsHub to search for or download a model, your search terms, model download requests, and limited technical information may be sent to Hugging Face in order to provide model search and download functionality.

FIL AI does not store your chats, prompts, AI responses, or personal information on servers controlled by FILIONROBOTICS. Your chats and data are kept locally within the app. When you use features that require Hugging Face, limited information may be sent to Hugging Face solely to provide that functionality.

## Use of Hugging Face

FIL AI uses Hugging Face to search for and download AI models for local use.

Information sent to Hugging Face may include the text you type into the ModelsHub search bar, model download requests, and limited technical information needed to complete those requests. You should not enter sensitive personal information, confidential information, passwords, financial information, health information, or other private data into the search bar.

Hugging Face’s own privacy and data handling practices apply to information processed by their services.

## Data Storage

FIL AI stores user chats locally on the user’s device so users can view, continue, and manage their conversations within the app.

FIL AI does not send user chats, prompts, or AI responses to FILIONROBOTICS, Hugging Face, or any other company for conversation processing. After an AI model is downloaded, conversations with that model are processed locally on the user’s device.

Users can delete their stored conversations at any time within the app. If Temporary Chat is enabled, conversations are not saved after the chat session ends.

## Children’s Privacy

FIL AI is intended for a general audience and may be used by users of different ages. FIL AI does not require users to create an account and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

AI-generated responses may be unpredictable and are generated by models running locally on the user’s device. FILIONROBOTICS does not control or guarantee every response generated by an AI model.

Children should use FIL AI with guidance from a parent or guardian, as AI-generated responses may sometimes be unpredictable.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

FILIONROBOTICS may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
